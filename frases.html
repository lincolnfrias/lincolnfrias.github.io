início


As frases abaixo estão em ordem de dificuldade.

As traduções estão ao final.



  1. The product price is too high.
  2. Is the product price too low?
  3. The profits are too low. The company will not survive.
  4. The monthly profits are decreasing.
  5. The yearly sales are increasing.
  6. The daily costs were stable.
  7. Traduza: Os lucros semanais estão muito baixos.
  8. Last year, the company wasted time with lawsuits.
  9. The company had many problems last year.
  10. Many customers were lost last year.
  11. The company lost too much customers on recent years.
  12. Next year, the sales will rise and the customers will come back.
  13. They are looking for new buyers for their products.
  14. Traduza: A empresa está procurando um comprador para seu produto.
  15. Old strategies are incapable of solving the recent problems.
  16. Young people are having problems finding jobs.
  17. Young people have problems to find jobs.
  18. The company developed a new product, but its price is still unacceptable for many of its older consumers, even with all the discounts given in the last round of negotiations.
  19. The company tried to create a strategy to reach the highest acceptable price.
  20. For some of them, calculus is not the hardest discipline in economics.
  21. Last week, researchers found evidence of inconsistencies in the financial statements of some of the larger Brazilian companies.
  22. College students should spend some of their time on community outreach activities.
  23. Given last quarter results, the stockholders are demanding some change in the strategy of the company.
  24. Our business is to improve people's lives.
  25. Their obligation is to fulfill their promises.
  26. We fulfilled two hundred orders today.
  27. You should now proceed to the fulfillment of this form.
  28. The customers’ satisfaction should be our priority.
  29. They complained about our service and about the condition of the floor.
  30. The number of complaints is constantly rising everywhere.
  31. Have you ever complained in a restaurant?
  32. Customers aren't accepting bad products anymore.
